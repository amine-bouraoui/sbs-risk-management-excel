# Risk Management Model — SBS Nettoyage

Advanced Excel risk management framework built for SBS Nettoyage, 
a real cleaning and security services company in Tunisia.

---

## Overview

A structured risk register covering 20 operational risks across 
5 weighted categories, with automated scoring, before/after 
mitigation analysis, and an auto-updating dashboard.

---

## Key Results

| Metric | Before Mitigation | After Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Overall Rating | **Severe** | **Moderate** |
| Critical Risks | 10 | 0 |
| Severe Risks | 3 | 6 |
| Moderate Risks | 7 | 7 |
| Sustainable Risks | 0 | 7 |

---

## Risk Categories & Weights

| Category | Weight | Pre-Mitigation | Post-Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Operational | 35% | Critical | Sustainable |
| Financial | 20% | Severe | Moderate |
| Legal | 20% | Moderate | Sustainable |
| Human | 20% | Moderate | Sustainable |
| Marketing | 5% | Severe | Moderate |

---

## Methodology

- **Risk scoring:** probability × impact matrix (5×5 scale — 
  Sustainable / Moderate / Severe / Critical)
- **Weighted composite scoring:** each category weighted by 
  business impact
- **Before/after mitigation:** every risk scored pre and 
  post-mitigation with assigned risk owners
- **Auto-updating dashboard:** risk levels recalculate 
  automatically when scores change
- **Color mapping:** consistent semantic color encoding 
  across all risk levels
